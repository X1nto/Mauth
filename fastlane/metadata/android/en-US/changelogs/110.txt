New Features
============
- You can now organize your accounts into groups
- Added account search, matching by label or issuer
- Added an auto-lock security setting to lock the app every time you re-enter it (available once PIN lock is enabled)

Changes
=======
- Revamped the home screen navigation
- Refined in-app controls to use Material 3 Expressive look
- Updated the about screen
- Action buttons now show tooltips on long press
- Refreshed the camera permission screen on the QR scanner
- Updated translations

Fixes
=====
- Content is no longer streched on large screens
- Minor performance and stability improvements
